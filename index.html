Who I Am

A photographer
based in Stockholm

Hi, I'm Ruban Kumar Shankar — a photographer based in Stockholm. My work focuses on portrait and street photography, with an interest in capturing mood, expressions, and quiet moments.

I'm drawn to images that feel natural and cinematic, often exploring calmness, solitude, and atmosphere. I photograph models, artists, couples, and creative individuals, and I'm available for editorial, portrait, and commissioned projects.

"I'm drawn to images that feel natural and cinematic — calmness, solitude, and atmosphere in every frame."
— Ruban Kumar Shankar
